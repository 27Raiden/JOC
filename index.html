Die Hard

Movie, 1988

Die Hard is about a cop named John McClane stuck in a big building with bad guys. He's trying to save everyone, including his wife. McClane fights the bad guys using his smarts and bravery. He talks to another cop outside to help him. In the end, McClane beats the bad guys and saves the day.

The Martian

Book, 2014

The Martian is a book written by Andy Weir in 2014. The plot follows an astronaut who was stranded on Mars. Instead of attempting to rescue him, the space program tries to cover up that someone got left behind. Overwhelmed by guilt, the other crew members attempt to rescue him. In the end, they are successful and bring him home.
